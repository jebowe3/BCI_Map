User Added Points

To add a point of interest, click a location on the map or enter the coordinates manually. For the year range, enter using the following format: 1920-1929. If the point refers to something that happened in one year, just type the relevant year (ex: 1925). Upon submission, reload the map. It may take a few minutes before the changes are logged and your point appears. Once verified, it will be added to the main map.
